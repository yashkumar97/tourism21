Monsoon Holiday

Monsoon holidays in India surely offer varied memorable experiences that are worth-cherishing for a lifetime. Showers bring respite from high temperatures. Contrary to popular belief, rainy season can be a wonderful time to explore this beautiful country. There are some spectacular places to visit in monsoon in India. Here are some of the top monsoon holiday destinations in India including Goa, Cherrapunji, Mahabaleshwar, Lonavala, Kodaikanal, Coorg, and Munnar, Periyar and Wayanad in Kerala. This is a wonderful time to reap the benefits of Ayurveda. Lahual and Spiti valley are delightful too at this time. Sightseeing in the Lake City of Udaipur is extremely popular. Monsoon increases the scenic beauty of all these places, which are already enchanting. Go through the details, take your pick, and plan your monsoon holiday now!
